# Car Buddy — Mobile Mechanic Marketplace + AI Car Assistant

**Status:** Idea stage — saved for later
**Date:** February 28, 2026
**Ties into:** Red Pine ecosystem (customer DB, marketing, email, social media, payments)

## The Problem
- Not everyone is a car person
- No easy marketplace for mobile mechanics (like DoorDash but for car repair)
- No simple app to track oil changes, tire rotations, battery health
- Searching Google for mechanics is hit-or-miss
- Electric cars add even more complexity (battery management, specialized service)

## The Idea

### Part 1: Car Buddy (AI Assistant)
- Simple onboarding: make, model, year of your car
- Tracks maintenance schedule (oil changes, tires, brakes, fluids)
- AI reminders: "Your oil change is due in 200 miles"
- Smart for YOUR specific car (Tesla battery vs F-150 diesel vs Honda Civic)
- Stores your service history, receipts, mechanic notes

### Part 2: Mobile Mechanic Marketplace
- Browse mechanics by location, rating, specialty
- See them on the go like DoorDash — real-time availability
- Book a mobile mechanic to come to you (flat tire, dead battery, roadside)
- Stuck on the road? Quick action to get help NOW
- Reviews, ratings, verified mechanics

### Part 3: Partner Providers
- Top partner mechanics featured at top of list
- Location-based recommendations
- Specialties (electric vehicles, European cars, trucks, etc.)

## How It Ties to Red Pine
- Customer database → Red Pine
- Email/SMS notifications → Red Pine comms
- Social media marketing → Red Pine Postiz
- Payment processing → Red Pine Stripe Connect
- Mechanic business management → they could use Red Pine OS for their own business
- Review management → Red Pine review system
